Simulation 2

After the virus has been taken off the surface it is time to destroy it. Here is where soap is crucial. It is gonna attach itself to the different components of the virus and split them apart.

In this second simulation we can see the different componets of the virus in a veri simplified way. If you want to see the virus more in detail and learn more things about it, go to our post.

Here we can see represented the lipidic membrane in light green, the proteins of the membrane in dark green and the contagious DNA represented as red dots linked between each others in the middle of the virus.

If we do nothing to the virus and we wait enough time, we will see how it is going to decompose extremley slowly. Just like would happen to a real virus in solution. It is going to decompose but the process is very very slow. Depending on the surface and the conditions that time varies but it can be needed up to 5 days for a coronavirus to decompose by itself.

But using soap we only need a few seconds to completly destroy all of them and clean almost any surface.

Now you know why it is so important to clean your hands and why you should also clean other things like groceries, your phone or your kitchen.
