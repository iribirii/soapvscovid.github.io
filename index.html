Soap vs COVID-19.

Learn how soap works and why it is so effective fighting against COVID-19 or any other virus. Visualize some soap molecules, learn about their different parts and how they interact the the different compounds around them.

  • Learn more...

Simulation 1

In this simulation you will have to take some virus particles off the surface they are attached to using the power of soap. Create soap particles that interact with the viruses and see how they get surrounded by them and navigate without destination through water.

  • Run simulation

Simulation 2

In this simulation you will completly destroy the viruses. After taking them off the surface now it is time to erradicate them. Now the soap is not surrounding the whole virus but its individual componentes. Destroy the capsule and liberate the DNA trapped inside.
